Hello!

I'm Laura, a freelance photographer and mother of eight wonderful
children. My journey into photography began when I became a mother, my
passion for photography deepened, and I found myself constantly
capturing my children's milestones and everyday moments.Eventually,
my love for photography led me to start my own company, Marvelous
Moments Photography. Through my business, I've had the privilege of
capturing the most precious moments in the lives of families, couples,
and individuals. Each shoot is a unique experience, and I strive to
bring out the personalities and emotions of my subjects in every image. As
a mother of eight fun and busy kiddos, I understand the importance
preserving memories. I'm so grateful to be able to
do what I love every day and to help families create lasting memories
that they can cherish for years to come. Let's create your marvelous
moment! ❤️
